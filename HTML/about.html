About Us

50+ Years of Serving the Muslim Community in Negombo

Our History

Negombo Grand Masjid was established in 1970 by a group of visionary community leaders from prominent Negombo families. What began as a small prayer space has grown into a network of 11 masjids serving over 500 families across Negombo.

1970

Foundation

Established by Hassan, Ismail, and Rizwan families with initial capacity for 100 worshippers

1985

First Expansion

Added Al-Madina Masjid in Kochchikade to serve growing community needs

2000

Network Growth

Expanded to 8 masjids across Negombo, serving 300+ families

2020

Modern Era

Now managing 11 masjids with digital services and community programs

Historical Photos

Our Mission

To serve as a spiritual, educational, and social center for the Muslim community in Negombo, preserving Islamic values while adapting to contemporary needs across our 11 masjids.

Our Vision

To be the leading Islamic institution in Sri Lanka, nurturing future generations while maintaining strong family traditions and community bonds across all our masjids.

Our Values

Faith, Family, Community, Education, Service, and Inclusivity - guiding principles that have sustained us for over 50 years across generations.

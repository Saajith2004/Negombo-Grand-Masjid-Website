About Negombo Grand Masjid

A legacy of unity, faith, and committed service to the Negombo Muslim community

Our History

The Negombo Grand Masjid stands as one of the oldest and most respected Islamic institutions in the region. For decades, it has served as a spiritual home for the Muslim community, offering religious guidance, education, and essential social welfare services.

Built on strong foundations of unity and community support, the Masjid continues to play an important role in Islamic education, charity, leadership development, and social service initiatives.

1950s

Early Foundations

Community leaders established a small prayer hall, which later expanded into a full Masjid with community support.

1980s

Education Programs Begin

The Masjid introduced Quran classes, Islamic studies, and weekend learning programs for children and adults.

About Bitulmahal

Our housing assistance program for the community

The Bitulmahal program is an initiative by Negombo Grand Masjid to provide housing assistance to families in need within our community. Established in 1985, this program has helped numerous families secure safe and dignified housing.

Housing Assistance

Financial support for home construction, repairs, and renovations

Community Support

Volunteer labor and material donations from community members

Family Focused

Priority given to families with children, elderly, and special needs

Application Process

How to apply for Bitulmahal assistance

1

Initial Inquiry

Contact our Bitulmahal committee or submit the online inquiry form

2

Documentation

Submit required documents including proof of need and property details

3

Home Visit

Our committee will conduct a home visit to assess the situation

4

Approval

Application reviewed and approved by the Bitulmahal committee

5

Implementation

Work begins with community volunteers and allocated funds

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about Bitulmahal program

Who is eligible for Bitulmahal assistance?

Muslim families residing in Negombo area with genuine housing needs, particularly those with limited income, elderly members, persons with disabilities, or large families living in inadequate conditions.

What types of housing assistance are provided?

We provide various forms of assistance including home construction for those without proper housing, home repairs and renovations, roof repairs, bathroom and kitchen improvements, and emergency housing support.

How long does the application process take?

The complete process typically takes 4-8 weeks from initial application to approval. Emergency cases may be processed faster. The timeline depends on the complexity of the case and availability of funds.

Can I volunteer for Bitulmahal projects?

Yes! We welcome volunteers with various skills - construction, plumbing, electrical work, painting, or general labor. You can also volunteer for administrative tasks or fundraising activities.
